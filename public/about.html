Our History

Where ancient Roman elegance meets modern luxury on the shore of the Mediterranean Sea

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1998, Antique Roman Palace was created as a place where modern comfort meets the spirit of majestic antiquity. Our hotel is inspired by the aesthetics of Ancient Rome: columns, marble statues, mosaic panels, and the atmosphere of a true seaside palace.

From the very first step into our lobby, guests enter a space where history comes to life — with an interior designed with attention to detail and respect for classical art. There are no random elements here: every niche, chandelier, and arch is chosen to convey a sense of grandeur, harmony, and serenity.

We are located in a picturesque part of Alanya, just a few steps from the Mediterranean Sea and close to the city’s cultural landmarks — the castle, the port, and Cleopatra Beach. Antique Roman Palace operates under the Adults Only concept and offers guests an atmosphere of private relaxation, where the sea, history, and service merge into one experience.
